Refund Policy

Return & Refund Guidelines

At Khadijah Cakes & Confectionaries, we stand behind the quality of our products. Please review our refund policy below:

Eligibility for Refunds (Due to the perishable nature of our products)

  • Refund requests must be submitted within 24 hours of purchase or delivery
  • Items found to be defective or damaged at the time of delivery or pickup.
  • Proof of purchase (order confirmation) is required

Non-Refundable Items

  • Custom-made cakes and personalized orders
  • Change of mind after the order has been prepared.
  • Damage occurred after the item has left our premises (for pickups).
  • Already consumed or partially consumed items
  • Incorrect storage or handling by the customer.

Refund Process

  1. Contact us via phone or email with your order details
  2. Provide photos of the product if applicable
  3. Our team will review and approve/decline within 48 hours
  4. Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days

Delivery Policy

Delivery Information & Terms

We deliver fresh, delicious orders right to your doorstep. Here's what you need to know about our delivery service:

Delivery Areas

  • Ikorodu and surrounding areas in Lagos
  • Standard delivery within our service radius
  • Special arrangements available for far locations

Delivery Times

  • Standard Delivery: 2-3 business days (Orders placed Mon-Fri, 10am-7pm)
  • Same-Day Delivery: Available for orders placed before 12:00 PM (Additional charges may apply)
  • Weekend Deliveries: Available upon request

Delivery Charges

  • Free delivery for orders above ₦100,000
  • Flat rate of ₦2,500 for orders below ₦80,000
  • Same-day rush delivery: Additional ₦1,500

Product Handling & Care

  • All items are properly packaged to ensure freshness
  • Cakes are delivered in insulated containers
  • Refrigerable items should be stored properly upon receipt

What to Do Upon Delivery

  1. Inspect your order immediately upon receipt
  2. Report any damages or discrepancies within 1 hour
  3. Keep your receipt for warranty/replacement claims

Special Orders & Events

For large orders or special event catering, please contact us directly at least 3 days in advance for personalized delivery arrangements.
